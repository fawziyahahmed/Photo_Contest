Categories

This contest includes 3 categories of photography: wildlife, food, and nature. Please take your time to explore the categories.

Wildlife

Wildlife traditionally refers to undomesticated animal species, but has come to include all organisms that grow or live wild in an area without being introduced by humans. Therefore, this category is not limited to animals, birds, and insects. Marin subjects and botanical subjects taken in the wild are suitable wildlife subjects, as are carcasses of extant species. Photographs of zoo or game farm animals, or of any extant zoological or botanical species taken under controlled conditions are not eligible in Wildlife sections.

Food

This categories include any food from restaurants, food that you have made, or your family has made. Do not include any type of beverages. Do not include yourself eating in the photo. You can include any type of spicy, sweet, and well designed food.

Nature

This category can include landscapes, geologic formations, weather phenomena, and extant organisms as the primary subject matter. This includes images taken with the subjects in controlled conditions, such as zoos, game farms, botanical gardens, aquariums and any enclosure where the subjects are totally dependent on man for food.
